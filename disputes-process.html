Sending and review of the claim

1. Sending a claim to TPBIA

  • First, a Claimant should submit a request to its broker directly. Only after this, the Claimant can submit a claim to TPBIA after a specified period of time since the day of the incident.
  • A Claimant can submit a claim to our Association if unsatisfied with the decision of a broker, or the reply was not provided within 5 workdays.

2. Claim analysis

  • We will review your claim and confirm whether it falls under our area of responsibility.
  • Next, we will analyze the situation, check all the provided materials, and consider the possibility of settling the dispute.

3. Review of the claim

  • The Association makes a decision based on all the information received during the analysis.
  • If there is no sufficient information for making a decision, the Association may request additional information from either Claimant and/or broker.

4. Decisions and Orders

  • If a Claimant does not agree with the decision of the Association, then it is necessary to reaffirm the opposition within 14 days from the moment of issuing the decision.
  • If a Claimant did not confirm its disagreement with the decision of the Association within the specified period, then the claim is considered as closed.
  • A member of the Association should fulfill the claim in accordance with the decision of the Association by the required deadline since the moment of claim resolution.
