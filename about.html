For Brokers and Traders

The brokers that become members of TPBIA are issued a certificate that confirms their membership in the Association. It helps to make interacton with traders simplier and more honest.

Our Association is made up of independent experts and professional financial advisors that efficiently solve all complicated claims and controversies with brokers.

We guarantee that each trader will receive the following things from TPBIA:
  • Reasoned response on their claims whatever the decision of TPBIA
  • Objective consideration and fair settlement of a dispute
  • Compensation from the TPBIA Fund if the claim is approved

About TPBIA Association

Trading Platforms and Brokers International Association is a self-regulated independent organization that performs settlement of disputes activities on the trading platforms between traders and brokers. The team of our Association is staffed by highly professional experts in finance and jurisprudence, that have excellent work experience in the resolution of disputes with brokers.

We guarantee fast and efficient resolution of disputes, transparency in communication with traders and brokers. The TPBIA professionals, as experts in this area, can objectively assess situations and competently resolve any disputes in online trading.
