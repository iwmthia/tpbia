Please, attend!

Our Association was informed that some fake representatives of TPBIA imitate our brand and services by using our logotype and similar email addresses. They fraudulently offer falsified services to traders.

We take all measures to inform traders about the fraudulent activities of fake representatives of our company. Some fraud schemes that are used by different fakes are hard to recognize even by very cautious investors.

In order to protect traders, we highly recommend to review the following:

  • Our services are free of charge for traders. We do not have any fees for traders and brokers with membership.
  • We do not consider claims to the brokers that are not the members of TPBIA.
  • We do not initiate contacts by ourselves with traders.
  • All Claimants should fill in the claim form in order to file a claim.
  • Check all contact details carefully in emails that you receive. We always use the protected HTTPS protocol and official email address.
  • If you've received an email from somebody saying that it is a TPBIA employee, and you did not file a claim or question before, please resend the received email to our official mail: info@tpbia.com.
  • Do not provide fraudsters any personal data.
